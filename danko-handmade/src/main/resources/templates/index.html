About the Artist

Greetings, I'm Danko, a storyteller in clay.

Born in Pleven, Bulgaria, my pottery journey began at my grandmother’s ceramics workshop, where I watched formless clay awaken into art. The kiln’s fiery alchemy, the graceful dance of skilled hands, and the gentle crackling of cooling pottery wove their spell around me.

It was love at first sight, and it stayed for life.
